Choropleth Mapping: Native Population in California Counties

In this choropleth map, the depiction of the Native American population in the counties of California is visualized. We can see the highest percentage of the demographic in the three blue counties; two that are located along the eastern border of the state and one at the very top. The region that contains counties that have a Native American population anywhere from 3% to 7% is locatedin the northern part of the state--colored in the dark green. The majority of the state, is in the light green classification, meaning 0% to 3% of the population identifies as Native American.

Bivariate Mapping: An Analysis of Average Income and the Baby Boomer in Los Angeles

This bivariate map depicts the relationship between the average household income and the block group's precentage of the generational class, Baby Boomers. Generally, we can see high values of both variables in the north-western half of the blockgroup. This is indicative of the the area been denser in richer households and having a larger percentage of Baby Boomer's residing in the block. Conversely, we can see a variety of the other classfication throughout the rest of the cartographic piece, such as the eastern half of the map having more counties that are depcited as having low average income and a high baby boomer population, as seen in cyan. We also have a distinct cluster of the white classifcation towards the center-right part of the map, indicating low average income and a low baby boomer population percentage.

Proportional Symbol Mapping: Daycare Openings in Iowa (April 2023)

In this proportional symbol map, we can see the raw values of daycare openings. Due to the implementation of raw totals, we can see this map essentially how us the highest populated areas of the sate of Iowa—namely, the largest clusters of daycares are in the areas of Des Moines, Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, the Quad Cities, Sioux City, and Waterloo. Overall, the presence of daycares is well dispersed though out the state, even if the individual businesses don’t have many openings. The eastern half of the states seem to have more daycares and openings than compared to the western hold. However, the daycare with the most openings is on the western portion, towards the top of the state.
